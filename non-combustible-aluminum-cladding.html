Non-Combustible Aluminum Cladding Panels

EN 13501-1 A2-s1,d0 certified non-combustible aluminum cladding panels for safe building facades worldwide.

A2-s1,d0 Fire Rating

Our panels achieve the highest fire safety rating for combustible building materials in the EU. A2-s1,d0 means: non-combustible core, no smoke production, no burning droplets.

Applications

  • High-rise building facades
  • Airports and railway stations
  • Hospitals and schools
  • Government buildings
  • Commercial complexes

Why Non-Combustible Cladding Matters

Building fires caused by combustible cladding have led to stricter regulations worldwide. Our A2 panels meet the most stringent fire safety standards, giving architects and developers peace of mind.
