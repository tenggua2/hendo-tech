A2 Fire Rated Aluminum Composite Panel Manufacturer

Jiangsu Hengdu Phase Change Technology Co., Ltd. is the world's first manufacturer of screw-extruded A2 non-combustible aluminum composite panels.

Why Choose Our A2 Fire Rated Panels?

Traditional A2 panels still absorb moisture, which compromises fire safety over time. Our proprietary screw-extrusion technology produces A2 core material with zero water absorption — maintaining consistent fire resistance throughout the panel's service life.
