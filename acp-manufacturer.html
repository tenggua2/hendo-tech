Aluminium Composite Panel Manufacturer — China Direct Factory

Hengdu Technology is a specialized aluminium composite panel (ACP) manufacturer based in Suqian, Jiangsu, China. We produce A2 fire-rated ACP with our proprietary zero water absorption screw-extrusion technology — a world first in the composite panel industry.

Why Choose Hengdu as Your ACP Manufacturer?

  • World's first screw-extruded A2 core — zero water absorption (<0.1%), no delamination
  • Full vertical integration — from A2 core granules to core rolls to finished panels, all in-house
  • 11 production lines — 8 granule lines (200 t/day), 1 core roll line (50 t/day), panel lines (5,000 sqm/day)
  • 6 international fire certifications — EN 13501-1, GB 8624, BS 476, ASTM E84, NFPA 285
  • Direct factory pricing — no middleman markup, manufacturer-direct quotation
  • Fast delivery — standard colors ship in 7 days, custom colors in 15 days
  • 5 invention patents — proprietary supramolecular coupling technology
