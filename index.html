螺杆挤出连续生产

Technology
Core Advantages
From lab to mass production — every advantage is an industry barrier
🧪

Supramolecular Coupling

Proprietary technology couples inorganic flame retardants with organic polymers at the molecular level, enabling screw extrusion at ≥90% mineral loading — a world first.

💧

Zero Water Absorption

Conventional A2 cores absorb 3%-7% moisture, causing ≥20% peel strength loss. Hengdu's extruded core has near-zero absorption. The composite panel maintains mechanical properties in any humidity.

⚡

Screw Extrusion Production

Extrusion speed >3 m/min vs conventional hot-press <2 m/min. Continuous process guarantees batch consistency. 50-ton daily core roll output for large orders.

♻️

100% Recyclable Waste

Production offcuts can be processed and reused in core extrusion — zero-waste manufacturing. At the customer's site, installation scrap also has recycling value.

🛡️

Complete Global Certifications

A2 non-combustible certified across all four major economic zones: China, EU, UK, and USA. One of very few ACP brands genuinely ready for global export.

🏭

Full Vertical Integration

From granules → core rolls → finished ACP — the entire chain is in-house. Controlled quality, predictable lead times, competitive cost. Buy any intermediate product as needed.
